Central Florida Entertainment Map

Michael Clark
Central Florida is a hotspot for tourist entertainment. Regardless of preference, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Growing up within minutes of central Florida attractions, you learn to pick up on what people like. Families with children would most likely enjoy Magic Kingdom. Thrill seekers and young adults are more inclined to visit Universal Studios. Overall, I feel like Sea World has a great balance of thrills like rollercoasters and rides while still appealing to shows and performances.
